Our Story

Bethesda House of Grace was founded in 1990 with a vision to create a community where people from all walks of life could encounter the love and healing of Jesus Christ. Named after the pool of Bethesda where Jesus performed miracles, our church stands as a symbol of healing, hope, and grace.

Over the decades, we have grown from a small group of 20 families to a vibrant congregation of over 1,000 members. We are committed to preaching the Gospel, making disciples, and serving our community.

🎯 Our Vision

To be a house of healing, hope, and transformation where lives are changed by the power of the Gospel.

🌟 Our Mission

To love God, love people, and make disciples of all nations through worship, fellowship, and service.

💎 Our Values

  • Faith in Jesus Christ
  • Love for One Another
  • Service to the Community
  • Excellence in Worship
